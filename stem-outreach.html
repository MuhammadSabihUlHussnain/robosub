School demos

Hattiesburg STEMBot Program

The team visits classrooms and youth programs to introduce students to underwater robots, ocean engineering, and the kind of design thinking used in RoboSub.

Students can ask questions, meet Southern Miss mentors, and practice piloting small vehicles through underwater obstacle challenges.

STEMBot Outreach Program

STEMBot can run as a multi-week after-school program where team members teach buoyancy, electrical basics, CAD thinking, software logic, and testing habits.

The goal is to let students move from a concept sketch to a small underwater vehicle they can actually test.
