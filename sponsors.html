Current sponsors

Organizations supporting RoboSub

Our sponsors help make hands-on autonomous systems work possible for Southern Miss students.

Defense technology and digital engineering

Integer Technologies

Integer Technologies transforms research into fieldable technology for national security and industry customers, with capabilities across unmanned systems, sensors and perception, power and energy systems, advanced manufacturing, and cyber-physical resilience.

Their Gulf Coast work with Southern Miss at the Roger F. Wicker Center for Ocean Enterprise supports autonomous systems in complex undersea environments, making their sponsorship a strong match for RoboSub student engineering.

Visit Integer Technologies

Public safety partner

Mississippi Highway Patrol

Mississippi Highway Patrol is part of the Mississippi Department of Public Safety and provides statewide highway enforcement, district response, traffic-law enforcement, motorist assistance, and public-safety support for local agencies.

Their support connects our engineering work with the discipline, reliability, and field readiness expected from public-safety operations across Mississippi.

Visit Mississippi Highway Patrol

Community partner

Petal Fire Department

Petal Fire Department serves the City of Petal as an all-risk, all-hazards department focused on fire suppression, fire prevention, emergency rescue, and reducing loss of life and property for residents and visitors.

The department's community-first mission mirrors the teamwork, safety mindset, and practical problem solving that guide our RoboSub build and testing process.

Visit Petal Fire Department

Fabrication partner

B&W Fabrications

B&W Fabrications is a Wesson, Mississippi metal fabrication company built around practical problem solving, dependable shop work, and custom fabricated parts for industrial customers.

Their support strengthens the hands-on manufacturing side of RoboSub, from frame hardware and brackets to the real build skills needed to turn a design into a pool-tested vehicle.
